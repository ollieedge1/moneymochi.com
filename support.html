Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan a receipt?

Tap the "Scan Receipt" button on the home screen, then point your camera at the receipt. Hold it steady and Supermonster will read the items automatically. You can edit anything after scanning.

Why did the scan miss some items?

Receipt scanning works best with clear, flat receipts in good lighting. If items are missing, you can add them manually using the edit feature. Crumpled or faded receipts may not scan as accurately.

How do I edit a scanned receipt?

Go to Spending > tap the receipt > tap the three-dot menu > Edit Receipt. You can change the store name, item names, categories, prices, and total. You can also add or remove items.

How does price tracking work?

When you scan the same item at the same store on different occasions, Supermonster automatically detects if the price has changed and shows you the difference in the Price Tracking section.

How do I set a budget?

On the home screen, tap "Set a budget" or use the menu on an existing budget card to add another one. You can name budgets whatever you like and set them as weekly or monthly.

How many free scans do I get?

Free users get 4 receipt scans per calendar month. Scans reset on the 1st of each month. Upgrade to Supermonster Pro for unlimited scanning.

How does household sharing work?

Go to Settings > Household to create or join a household. Once linked, household members can see shared spending data, shopping lists, and budgets. Each member keeps their own account and monster.

What is "Help improve price data"?

If you opt in, Supermonster shares anonymised, aggregated shopping data to help build better price insights. No personal information is ever shared — your name, email, and individual purchase history stay private. You can change this anytime in Settings > Data & Privacy.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all associated personal data within 30 days. This action cannot be undone.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Receipt images stay on your device — only the extracted text is stored. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
